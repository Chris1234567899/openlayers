--- layout: example.html title: Extent Interaction shortdesc: Using an Extent interaction to draw an extent. docs: >

This example shows how to use an Extent interaction to draw a modifiable extent.

Use Ctrl+Drag (Command+Drag on Mac) to draw an extent. Ctrl+Drag (Command+Drag on Mac) on the corners or edges of the extent to resize it. Ctrl+Click (Command+Click on Mac) off the extent to remove it.
